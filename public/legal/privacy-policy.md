# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 18, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

dshipit Inc. ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information in connection with our Services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, business information when you create an account
- **TikTok Shop Connection**: When you authorize dshipit to access your TikTok Shop account, we receive and store authentication credentials
- **Product Data**: Product listings, pricing, inventory information, and sales metrics from your TikTok Shop
- **Communication Data**: Email messages, support tickets, and chat communications

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with our Services (pages visited, features used, time spent)
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device type, IP address
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies**: We use cookies to improve your experience and analyze usage patterns
- **Log Data**: Server logs containing access times, pages visited, and error information

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **TikTok Shop**: Product information, shop metrics, and user engagement data through authorized API access
- **Analytics Providers**: Aggregated usage statistics and performance metrics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Services**: Deliver product research, sourcing recommendations, and analytics features
- **Authenticate Access**: Verify your identity and maintain secure account access
- **Improve Services**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality and user experience
- **Communications**: Send service updates, security alerts, and support responses
- **Compliance**: Fulfill legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
- **Fraud Prevention**: Detect and prevent unauthorized access or misuse
- **Analytics**: Understand user behavior and optimize platform performance

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 What We Do NOT Do

We do **not** sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 4.2 How We May Share Information

Your information may be shared with:

- **Service Providers**: Third-party vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, customer support, and payment processing
- **TikTok Shop**: Authentication tokens and API access credentials required to retrieve your authorized product data
- **Legal Compliance**: When required by law, court order, or government agency
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of merger, acquisition, or asset sale
- **Your Explicit Consent**: When you specifically authorize sharing of your information

### 4.3 TikTok Data Processing

When you connect your TikTok Shop account to dshipit:

- Your authentication credentials are encrypted and securely stored
- We access only the data necessary to provide Services (product listings, pricing, sales metrics)
- We comply with TikTok's Data Processing Agreement and Developer Terms
- TikTok data is subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy: [https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/eea/privacy-policy/en](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/eea/privacy-policy/en)

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Authentication**: OAuth 2.0 authentication for third-party integrations
- **Access Controls**: Restricted access to personal information based on job function
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Incident Response**: Procedures to respond to potential data breaches

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide Services to you
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
- Protect against fraudulent activity

When you close your account or revoke TikTok access:

- Authentication credentials are deleted immediately
- Your product data is deleted within 30 days unless you export it
- Account information is retained for 1 year for compliance and dispute resolution, then securely deleted

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### 7.1 European Economic Area (GDPR)

- **Right of Access**: Request a copy of your personal information
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your information ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your information
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object**: Withdraw consent for certain processing activities
- **Right to Lodge a Complaint**: Contact your local data protection authority

### 7.2 California (CCPA)

- **Right to Know**: Know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of personal information (with exceptions)
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information
- **Right to Correct**: Correct inaccurate information
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: No discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

### 7.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

**Email**: privacy@dshipit.com
**Mail**: dshipit Inc., Legal Department, [Your Address]
**Response Time**: We will respond to verified requests within 30 days (or as required by law)
